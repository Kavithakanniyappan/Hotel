Our Story

Good Food, Made Fresh Every Day

Kalakkal Kitchen Hotel is your trusted family hotel on Vandavasi to Chetpet Road, Kozhipuliyur X Road — right near Suraj School. We are known for quality food, generous portions, and a warm welcome.

From crispy dosas and biriyani to kothu parotta and fresh juices — everything is made fresh daily with traditional Tamil recipes. We believe good food brings good mood!
