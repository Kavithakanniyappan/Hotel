Our Story

Good Food, Made Fresh Every Day

Kalakkal Kitchen Hotel is your trusted roadside family hotel on Vandavasi to Chetpet Road, Kozhipuliyur X Road — right near Suraj School. Quality food, fresh every afternoon.

From crispy dosas and biriyani to kothu parotta and hot tea — everything made daily with traditional Tamil recipes and the best fresh ingredients. Open 1 PM to 11 PM every day.
